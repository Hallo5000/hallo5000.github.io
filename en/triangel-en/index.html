Triangel

The “Triangel” is our house bar, and is open every monday from 9 o’clock in the evening during the lecture period! During the non-lecture period we’re normally closed, except there is a special event. The Triangel maintains its  own little place on the web, where you can find event dates, current happenings, our newsletter and some more!
